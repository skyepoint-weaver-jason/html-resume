My hobbies

Passions

  • Yoga - my partner and I own a Hatha Yoga Studio called Comfortable Steady Yoga - Ashburn in Ashburn Virginia USA

    I started yoga after years of studying martial arts and QiGong. I reached a point where the Martial Arts, even Tai Chi, felt to violent for me. I wanted to retain the physical aspects but even more importantly were the mental, emotional and energetic aspects of the practice. I tried Bikram Yoga and Really liked that, but the warm-up, practice in 103 degree studio and cooldown required to much time for my schedule. I sought out other Yoga and Meditation Studios and eventually discovered Traditional Hatha Yoga as the best practice for me.
